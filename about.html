Our Story

Welcome. Let the world outside the doors fall away, for you have entered Pō. Before you is not just a menu, but a map of the story behind Pō. We have reached back into volcanic soil of the islands to bring you these offerings. Every grain of spice and every drop of nectar carries the breath of those who came before. Let the gods guide your path, just like the time before the sails, when the first gods cast stars into the dark.

When guests cross the threshold, they give themselves permission to leave the outside world behind. They are received by the warm glow of tiki torches and the intoxicating scent of exotic spices. The air is thick with anticipation as they step into a realm where time seems to stand still. The walls are adorned with intricate carvings and vibrant murals that tell stories of ancient Polynesian legends, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of culture and mythology.
